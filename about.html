About AssignmentHub

AssignmentHub is a revolutionary platform developed by a group of first-year students from PVG's COE.

Our mission is to provide a centralized hub for college students across India to easily access their assignments and valuable resources.

We believe in empowering students to excel in their academic journey and revolutionize the way assignments are managed.

AssignmentHub is an anonymous project, and the creators have chosen to remain undisclosed to avoid any potential consequences.
